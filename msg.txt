feat(kost): create detail view with modern editorial aesthetic

Implemented the KostDetail Livewire component and its corresponding blade
view to display property details elegantly. The UI features a clean,
monochromatic layout with a bold typography approach, aligning with the
'Modern Editorial Real Estate' style.

information section (badges, facilities, rules), and a sticky right
action card (which adapts to a floating bottom bar on mobile) equipped
with prominent Call-To-Action buttons for WhatsApp and internal messaging.
